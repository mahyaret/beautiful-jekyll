--- layout: post title: LED Blink ATmega32 date: '2010-04-05T15:20:00.000-04:00' author: Mahyar tags: - AVR modified_time: '2010-04-05T15:25:06.280-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-727803219276813688.post-2604717569389637458 blogger_orig_url: http://www.etedal.net/2010/04/led-blink-atmega32.html --- here is the bascom code :

$regfile = "m32def.dat"
$crystal = 8000000
Config Portd = Output
Do
Portd.6 = 0
Waitms 500
Portd.6 = 1
Waitms 500
Loop
End

pin10 ---> vcc
pin11 ---> gnd
pin20 ---> led+resistor(470 ohm)
