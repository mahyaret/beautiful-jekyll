--- layout: post title: Skype problem with Camera in Linux date: '2018-10-21T23:53:00.000-04:00' author: Mahyar tags: - Linux - debian modified_time: '2018-10-21T23:53:11.245-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-727803219276813688.post-7570979352333025197 blogger_orig_url: http://www.etedal.net/2018/10/skype-problem-with-camera-in-linux.html --- Suppose your webcam is in /dev/video0.


1. sudo apt-get install v4l2loopback-dkms

2. sudo modprobe v4l2loopback

3. Finally, when you need your webcam on Skype, just run this command:

    ffmpeg -i /dev/video0 -vcodec rawvideo -pix_fmt yuv420p -threads 0 -f v4l2 /dev/video1
